Breathe Better at Home
Indoor air can harbor pollutants at levels that may surprise you — often exceeding those found outdoors. That's why it's important to properly filter the air in your home.

Did you know when choosing a HVAC air filter, it's helpful to understand its Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) ratings? MERV indicates a filter's effectiveness, with higher ratings providing better filtration. Filters rated between 8 and 13 capture allergens, while those above 13 can tackle smaller particles.

Looking for a better solution? Consider polarized media air cleaners from Aire Serv®. These advanced filters combine high MERV ratings with electrical charges that attract and trap even more airborne contaminants.
